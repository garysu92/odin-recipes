Steak

Steak:

Cook in oven at 350 f for 40 minutes and then sear with garlic thyme rosemary butter.

    Ingredients
  • thyme
  • butter
  • oil
  • garlic
  • rosemary
  • salt
  • pepper
    Steps
  1. season steak w pepper and salt and let sit for 30 min in room temp
  2. preheat oven to 350 f
  3. put steak in and let cook for 40 min
  4. take out and let sit in room temp for 10 min
  5. sear both sides and cook in garlic thyme rosemary and butter
